This Plugin pulls facebook posts for you at any time you want to.

Currently the plugin works as follow:
1. You should download, install and then activate the plugin
2. After activating your plugin 
	(a) Go to Add New Post on your wordpress dashboard
	(b) Add whatever heding that you want for your post
	(c) copy the following shortcode 
		[fb-post href=""]
		replace the quotation marks after 'href' with the facebook post URL that you want to have on your blog
		
To find your facebook post URL, go to the post that you want
	(a) Then look for a date on the last comments of the post
	(b) Click on that date and then after that copy the whole link in your browser
